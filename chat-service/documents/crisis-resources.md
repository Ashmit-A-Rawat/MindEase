# Crisis Resources

If someone is in crisis or experiencing thoughts of self-harm, immediate professional help is the right next step — this is beyond what any chatbot conversation can safely resolve on its own.

## Immediate Help
- National Suicide Prevention Lifeline (US): Call or text 988, available anytime
- Crisis Text Line: Text HOME to 741741
- iCall (India): +91 9152987821, Monday-Saturday, 8am-10pm
- Vandrevala Foundation Helpline (India): 1860-2662-345 or 1800-2333-330, 24/7
- In immediate danger: contact local emergency services or go to the nearest emergency room

## What Good Support Looks Like in the Moment
- Take any mention of self-harm or suicide seriously — never dismiss it as attention-seeking
- Don't leave the person alone if they are in immediate danger
- Encourage contacting a crisis line or trusted person immediately rather than waiting
- Avoid trying to "fix" the situation alone — this is a moment for professional and emergency support, not amateur counseling
- Follow up afterward; a crisis moment passing doesn't mean the underlying issue is resolved

## MindEase's Role
MindEase's AI Support chat is designed for everyday coping support, psychoeducation, and pointing toward the right resources — not crisis intervention. Any message indicating active suicidal ideation, a plan for self-harm, or intent to harm others is met with an immediate, unconditional redirect to crisis resources, bypassing normal conversation entirely.
